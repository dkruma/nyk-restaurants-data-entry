Project Highlights

Data Accuracy

100% verified information with direct sourcing from TripAdvisor and restaurant official websites. All contact details manually validated.

Meeting-Focused

Curated list specifically targeting restaurants suitable for business meetings, ensuring professional atmosphere and amenities.

Quality Verified

All restaurants have substantial reviews (59-3,852 reviews), indicating established reputation and reliability for business use.

Data Collection Process

1

Source Identification

Accessed TripAdvisor's NYC restaurant database and filtered for establishments tagged as "good for meetings".

2

Data Extraction

Manually extracted restaurant names, addresses, and review counts from TripAdvisor listings.

3

Contact Information Research

Visited each restaurant's official website to gather accurate phone numbers, email addresses, and website URLs.

4

Data Validation

Cross-referenced all information to ensure accuracy and consistency across multiple sources.

5

Data Organization

Organized all collected data into structured Excel/Google Sheets format with proper formatting and categorization.
