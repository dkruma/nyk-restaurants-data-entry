Project Highlights

Data Accuracy

All data verified from TripAdvisor with accurate ratings and review counts. Information cross-referenced with restaurant official websites.

Meeting-Focused

Curated list specifically targeting restaurants suitable for business meetings, ensuring professional atmosphere and amenities.

Quality Verified

All restaurants have substantial reviews (59-3,852) with ratings from 4.1 to 5.0 stars, indicating established reputation for business use.

Data Collection Process

1

Source Identification

Accessed TripAdvisor's NYC restaurant database and filtered for establishments tagged as "good for meetings".

2

Data Extraction

Manually extracted restaurant names, addresses, ratings, and review counts from TripAdvisor listings with high attention to detail.

3

Website Verification

Visited and verified each restaurant's official website URL to ensure accuracy and accessibility for business inquiries.

4

Data Validation

Cross-referenced all information to ensure accuracy and consistency across multiple sources.

5

Data Organization

Organized all collected data into structured Excel/Google Sheets format with professional formatting and privacy compliance.
